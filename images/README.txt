HOW TO ADD YOUR 10 IMAGES
=========================

1. Put your pictures in THIS folder (the "images" folder).

2. Name them exactly like this:
   image-1.jpg
   image-2.jpg
   image-3.jpg
   ...
   image-10.jpg

3. If your files are .png or .webp instead of .jpg, open script.js
   and change the extension in the IMAGE_PATHS section.

4. Refresh the page in your browser to see them.

TIP: Use similar sizes (portrait photos work best) for a nice grid layout.
